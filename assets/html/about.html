    Experience:

    My Personal Training clientele and specialties stretch across a broad spectrum. My athletic background has allowed me to train athletes as young as 6 years old to 18 years old. These sports included baseball, basketball, volleyball, swimming and track. All athletes underwent power and strength training to maximize performance. Previous clients also include prehabilitation and flexibility. Who performed ROM assessment paired with various flexibility techniques to improve and prevent injury. Lastly, the large portion of past clientele is general health and fitness. General health and fitness is the gateway into living a better life. Your health and fitness are something that you cannot take for granted and will allow you to live every day to the fullest.

    Philosophy:

    Setting a goal that involves your health and fitness is a big step. As your trainer, I will make your health and fitness journey as seamless as possible. Together, we will design a personalized program that is functional and realistic to your lifestyle. I will hold you accountable while giving you advice and encouragement along the way. You will be given all the tools for success, but it is up to you to put in the effort!
